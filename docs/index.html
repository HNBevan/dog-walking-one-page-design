We Treat Your Dog
As One Of Ours.

When you hand us the lead, you're not hiring a service, you're introducing us to a member of your family. We take that introduction seriously. Every dog gets one named, dedicated walker who learns their routine, their quirks, and the things that make them anxious or excited, the same way we'd want to be known by someone looking after our own.

Walks are scheduled around your dog's energy, not a route algorithm. Every visit is logged and photographed, so you're never left guessing what happened between the front door and the front door. Full public liability insurance and DBS-checked walkers are standard here, not an upsell.

No rotating cast of strangers. No app dispatching whoever's nearest. Just the same person, getting to know your dog the way we'd want someone to know ours.

Tell Us About Your Dog.

Every relationship starts with a free home consultation. Before we meet, it helps to know the basics: your dog's name, breed, and age, their usual walking routine, and anything that makes them anxious, reactive, or excitable around other dogs. If they're on medication or have specific feeding instructions, mention that too. Fill in what you can and we'll cover the rest when we visit.

What we'll ask for

  • Your name and best contact number
  • Your dog's breed and age
  • Their usual routine and any quirks
